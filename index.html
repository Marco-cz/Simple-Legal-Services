Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?

Most legal departments are up and running in 4–6 weeks. Our onboarding team handles data migration, integrations, and user training so your team can focus on the work that matters.

Do you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes. Simple Legal Services connects with leading ERP, finance, and document management platforms including SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, SharePoint, and iManage out of the box.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, ISO 27001 compliant, and host all client data in encrypted, geographically redundant data centers with 24/7 monitoring.

What kind of support do you offer?

Every customer gets a dedicated success manager, 24/5 live chat, and access to our knowledge base. Enterprise plans include 24/7 priority support with a 1-hour response SLA.

Can we customize workflows for our team?

Yes. Workflows, approval chains, custom fields, and reporting dashboards can all be tailored without code through our admin console.
